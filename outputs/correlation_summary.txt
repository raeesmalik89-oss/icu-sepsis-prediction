
SEPSIS FLAG CORRELATION ANALYSIS - SUMMARY

Dataset: 15,000 ICU patients
Sepsis flag positive: 3,029 (20.2%)
Overall mortality: 3,418 (22.8%)

Key Findings:
1. Sepsis flag correlation with death: 0.093 (very weak)
2. Patients with sepsis flag die at 30.5% vs 20.8% without
3. Vital signs show NO difference between sepsis flag groups
4. Mortality prediction is more clinically valuable

Conclusion:
mortality_label will be the prediction target for this project.
