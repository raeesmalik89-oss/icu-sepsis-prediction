INTERPRETATION
============================================

1. Accuracy: Percentage of correct predictions overall
2. Precision: When model predicts death, how often correct
3. Recall: Percentage of actual deaths the model found
4. F1 Score: Balance between precision and recall
5. ROC-AUC: Model's ability to distinguish survivors from non-survivors

BEST MODEL SELECTION
============================================

The model with highest ROC-AUC will be selected for production.
This model will be saved as models/best_model.pkl
